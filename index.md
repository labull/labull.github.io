Lawrence is a Research Associate at The Alan Turing Institute in the [Data-Centric Engineering](https://www.turing.ac.uk/research/research-programmes/data-centric-engineering) programme, affiliated with the [Computational Statistics and Machine Learning Group](https://csml-cam.github.io) at the University of Cambridge. His research considers probabilistic methods for performance and health monitoring, focussing on systems-level digital twins and partially supervised inference. Previously he worked on statistical tools for the development of structural health monitoring in the Dynamics Research Group at the University of Sheffield.
